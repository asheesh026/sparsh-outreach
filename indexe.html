The Defence Pensioner Outreach and Grievance Redressal Program "Meerut Ka Sparsh" is being organized under the aegis of the Controller General of Defence Accounts, Ministry of Defence, on 1st May at the premises of the Controller of Defence Accounts (Army) office located in the cantonment area. During this program, awareness will be spread regarding the various services available on the SPARSH portal. Additionally, issues related to pension payments, document errors, and service record discrepancies of pensioners/family pensioners (Veer Naris) will be addressed.
